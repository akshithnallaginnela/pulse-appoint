## Getting Started

### 1. Open the Chatbot

Click the floating blue button with a chat icon in the bottom-right corner of the screen.

### 2. Start Chatting

The chatbot will greet you and offer suggestions. You can:
- Click a suggestion chip
- Type your message
- Use voice input (microphone icon)

### 3. Common Tasks

#### Book an Appointment

**Example conversations:**

```
You: I need to book an appointment
Bot: I'd be happy to help! What type of doctor are you looking for?
You: Cardiologist
Bot: [Shows cardiologist cards]
```

Or be more specific:
```
You: Book appointment with Dr. Sharma
Bot: [Shows Dr. Sharma's details and asks for date]
```

#### Find Doctors

```
You: Find a pediatrician
Bot: [Shows available pediatricians with ratings and fees]
```

#### View Your Appointments

```
You: Show my appointments
Bot: [Displays your upcoming appointments]
```

#### Cancel or Reschedule

```
You: Cancel my appointment
Bot: [Shows your appointments to select which one to cancel]
```

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Clear** - State what you want clearly (e.g., "book appointment", "find doctor")
2. **Use Suggestions** - Click the suggestion chips for quick actions
3. **Provide Details** - Mention specialization, doctor name, or dates when you know them
4. **Voice Input** - Click the microphone icon and speak clearly
5. **Be Patient** - The AI may take a few seconds to respond

## Voice Input

### Supported Browsers
- Google Chrome
- Microsoft Edge
- Opera

### How to Use
1. Click the microphone icon
2. Allow microphone access when prompted
3. Speak your message clearly
4. The text will appear in the input field
5. Click send or press Enter

## Privacy & Security

- All conversations are encrypted
- Your data is never shared with third parties
- Conversations are tied to your account
- You can end a session anytime by closing the chatbot

## Troubleshooting

### Chatbot not responding?
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Try logging out and back in

### Voice input not working?
- Ensure you're using Chrome or Edge
- Allow microphone permissions
- Check your microphone is working

### Getting unexpected responses?
- Try rephrasing your question
- Be more specific about what you need
- Use the suggestion chips

## Need Help?

If the chatbot can't help with your request, you can:
- Use the main navigation to access features directly
- Contact support through the Help section
- Call our support hotline
